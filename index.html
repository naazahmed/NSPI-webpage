ABOUT US

India is a country filled with unbound potential, where its 1/3rd population is students. Currently about 4 crore students are studying in the higher education institutions of the country. To lead the country towards rapid economic growth it is essential to provide Indian students with the proper infrastructure for business development, training and an atmosphere to improve their business competencies in a healthy manner. The purpose of this competition is to identify 100 talented student entrepreneurs along the country and to nourish them with essential skils through a residential business training.

Selection Process

From the comprehensive applications 100 ideas will be selected by a 5-member panel of juries of experts and practitioners. Later the best 4 will be selected by the jury from the residential camps by their overall performance and the clear understanding about the idea.

How to apply

The application form is prepared in a manner that it gives clear and complete details of the idea of the applicant. The applicants are expected to fill all the particulars and absence of the proposed details will lead to the disqualification of the application.
